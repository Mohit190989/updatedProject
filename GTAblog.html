GTA:GRAND THEFT AUTO

        Here's the information about the Grand Theft Auto (GTA) series:

        Overview:
        
        - Game Series: Grand Theft Auto (GTA)
        - Genre: Action-Adventure, Open-World, Crime
        - Developer: Rockstar North (formerly DMA Design)
        - Publisher: Rockstar Games
        - First Release: October 21, 1997 (GTA)
        - Latest Release: September 17, 2013 (GTA V)
        
        Games in the Series:
        
        1. Grand Theft Auto (1997)
        2. Grand Theft Auto 2 (1999)
        3. Grand Theft Auto III (2001)
        4. Grand Theft Auto: Vice City (2002)
        5. Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas (2004)
        6. Grand Theft Auto IV (2008)
        7. Grand Theft Auto: The Lost and Damned (2009)
        8. Grand Theft Auto: The Ballad of Gay Tony (2009)
        9. Grand Theft Auto V (2013)
        10. Grand Theft Auto Online (2013)
        
        Storyline:
        
        - The series follows various protagonists as they rise through the ranks of organized 
          crime in fictional cities.
        - Each game's storyline is standalone, but some games share characters and locations.
        
        Gameplay:
        
        - The series is known for its open-world gameplay, allowing players to explore and 
          interact with the game world.
        - Players can complete missions, engage in side activities, and participate in various
           criminal activities.
        
        Features:
        
        - The series has introduced various features, including 3D graphics (GTA III), online
           multiplayer (GTA IV), and a dynamic weather system (GTA V).
        - The games often feature real-world parody and satire, with humorous and critical 
          commentary on modern society.
        
        Development and Company:
        
        - Rockstar North is the primary developer of the series, with Rockstar Games publishing.
        - Rockstar Games was founded in 1998, and is known for its controversial and critically
          acclaimed games.
        
        Users and Popularity:
        
        - The GTA series has sold over 275 million copies worldwide.
        - The games have received critical acclaim for their storytelling, gameplay, and open-world design.
        
        Awards and Recognition:
        
        - The series has won numerous awards, including several Game of the Year awards.
        - GTA V won the "Game of the Year" award at the 2013 Spike Video Game Awards.
        
        Impact and Legacy:
        
        - The GTA series has had a significant impact on the gaming industry, influencing other 
          open-world and action-adventure games.
        - The series has also spawned various controversies and debates about video game
          violence and content.
