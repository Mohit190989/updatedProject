ASSASSIN'S CREED GAMES: IN ORDER
 Overview:

- Game Series: Assassin's Creed
- Genre: Action-Adventure, Stealth, Open-World
- Developer: Ubisoft Montreal (main developer), other Ubisoft studios
- Publisher: Ubisoft
- First Release: November 13, 2007 (Assassin's Creed)
- Latest Release: October 7, 2022 (Assassin's Creed Mirage)

Games in the Series:

1. Assassin's Creed (2007)
2. Assassin's Creed II (2009)
3. Assassin's Creed: Brotherhood (2010)
4. Assassin's Creed: Revelations (2011)
5. Assassin's Creed III (2012)
6. Assassin's Creed IV: Black Flag (2013)
7. Assassin's Creed: Unity (2014)
8. Assassin's Creed: Rogue (2014)
9. Assassin's Creed: Syndicate (2015)
10. Assassin's Creed: Origins (2017)
11. Assassin's Creed: Odyssey (2018)
12. Assassin's Creed: Valhalla (2020)
13. Assassin's Creed: Mirage (2022)

Storyline:

- The series follows the eternal conflict between the Assassins and the Templars, two ancient 
 secret societies.
- The games typically feature a modern-day protagonist who uses a device called the Animus to 
 relive the memories of their ancestors, who were Assassins.
- The series explores various historical settings, including the Crusades, Renaissance Italy, 
 Colonial America, and Ancient Greece.

Gameplay:

- The series is known for its open-world exploration, parkour mechanics, and stealth gameplay.
- Players can choose to complete objectives stealthily or through open combat.
- The games feature a variety of weapons, including swords, bows, and guns.

Features:

- The series has introduced various features over the years, including naval combat (Black Flag)
 , a dynamic weather system (Unity), and a dialogue choice system (Odyssey).
- The games often feature historical figures and events, such as Leonardo da Vinci, Napoleon 
 Bonaparte, and the American Revolution.

Development and Company:

- Ubisoft Montreal is the main developer of the series, with other Ubisoft studios contributing
  to individual games.
- Ubisoft was founded in 1986 and is headquartered in Montreuil, France.

Users and Popularity:

- The Assassin's Creed series has sold over 200 million copies worldwide.
- The games have received critical acclaim for their storytelling, gameplay, and historical 
 settings.

Awards and Recognition:

- The series has won numerous awards, including several Game of the Year awards.
- Assassin's Creed II won the "Game of the Year" award at the 2009 Spike Video Game Awards.

Impact and Legacy:

- The Assassin's Creed series has had a significant impact on the gaming industry, influencing
  other open-world and action-adventure games.
- The series has also spawned various spin-offs, including novels, comics, and a feature film.
