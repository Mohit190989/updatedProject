CALL OF DUTY

        Overview:

        - Game Series: Call of Duty
        - Genre: First-Person Shooter
        - Developer: Infinity Ward, Treyarch, Sledgehammer Games, and others
        - Publisher: Activision
        - First Release: October 29, 2003 (Call of Duty)
        - Latest Release: November 16, 2023 (Call of Duty: Modern Warfare III)
        
        Games in the Series:
        
        1. Call of Duty (2003)
        2. Call of Duty 2 (2005)
        3. Call of Duty: Big Red One (2005)
        4. Call of Duty 3 (2006)
        5. Call of Duty 4: Modern Warfare (2007)
        6. Call of Duty: World at War (2008)
        7. Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 (2009)
        8. Call of Duty: Black Ops (2010)
        9. Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 3 (2011)
        10. Call of Duty: Black Ops II (2012)
        11. Call of Duty: Ghosts (2013)
        12. Call of Duty: Advanced Warfare (2014)
        13. Call of Duty: Black Ops III (2015)
        14. Call of Duty: Infinite Warfare (2016)
        15. Call of Duty: Modern Warfare Remastered (2016)
        16. Call of Duty: WWII (2017)
        17. Call of Duty: Black Ops 4 (2018)
        18. Call of Duty: Modern Warfare (2019)
        19. Call of Duty: Black Ops Cold War (2020)
        20. Call of Duty: Vanguard (2021)
        21. Call of Duty: Modern Warfare II (2022)
        22. Call of Duty: Modern Warfare III (2023)
        
        Storyline:
        
        - The series primarily focuses on World War II and modern-day conflicts, with some 
         games exploring futuristic and sci-fi settings.
        - The games often feature multiple storylines and characters, with some titles connected
          by a larger narrative.
        
        Gameplay:
        
        - The series is known for its fast-paced, action-packed gameplay, with an emphasis on
          multiplayer modes.
        - Players can choose from various game modes, including Team Deathmatch, Domination, and
          Search and Destroy.
        
        Features:
        
        - The series has introduced various features over the years, including the "Create-a-
         Class" system (Black Ops), the "Pick 10" system (Black Ops II), and the "Gunsmith" 
          system (Modern Warfare).
        - The games often feature real-world weapons, vehicles, and equipment.
        
        Development and Company:
        
        - Infinity Ward, Treyarch, and Sledgehammer Games are the primary developers of the 
         series.
        - Activision was founded in 1979 and is headquartered in Santa Monica, California.
        
        Users and Popularity:
        
        - The Call of Duty series has sold over 300 million copies worldwide.
        - The games have received critical acclaim for their multiplayer modes, graphics, and
          sound design.
        
        Awards and Recognition:
        
        - The series has won numerous awards, including several Game of the Year awards.
        - Call of Duty 4: Modern Warfare won the "Game of the Year" award at the 2007 Spike
          Video Game Awards.
        
        Impact and Legacy:
        
        - The Call of Duty series has had a significant impact on the gaming industry, 
         influencing other first-person shooter games.
        - The series has also spawned various spin-offs, including novels, comics, and a 
         feature film.
