FREE FIRE
    Game Name: Garena Free Fire

    Release Date: September 30, 2017 (initial release), December 4, 2017 (global release)

    Company: Garena (published by), 111dots Studio (developed by), Sea Ltd (parent company)

    Origin: Singapore (Garena's headquarters)

    Users: Over 1 billion downloads on the Google Play Store alone,
           with an estimated 150-200 million monthly active users worldwide.
        
    Additional information:
        
    - Free Fire is a popular battle royale game for mobile devices, similar to PUBG Mobile
      and Fortnite.
    - The game has a smaller map and faster gameplay compared to other battle royale games,
      making it more suitable for mobile devices.
    - Free Fire has received numerous updates and collaborations with popular franchises
      like McLaren, Ferrari, and Marvel.
    - The game is available for both Android and iOS devices.

    GAMEPALY:

- Free Fire is a battle royale game where players are dropped on a remote island with up to
  49 other players.
- The objective is to be the last person or team standing, scavenging for weapons and supplies
  while navigating the map.
- The game has various modes, including Solo, Duos, and Squads, as well as special modes like
  Clash Squad and Bomb Squad.

Features:

- The game has a unique character system, with each character having their own abilities 
 and playstyles.
- Players can customize their characters with various outfits, accessories, and emotes.
- Free Fire has a variety of vehicles, including cars, motorcycles, and boats, which can
  be used for transportation and combat.
- The game also features a ranking system, with players earning points and badges as they 
 progress.

Company and Development:

- Garena is a Singaporean company founded in 2009, with a focus on online gaming and esports.
- 111dots Studio is a Vietnamese game development studio that developed Free Fire in 
 collaboration with Garena.
- Sea Ltd is the parent company of Garena, a multinational consumer internet company founded 
 in 2009.

Users and Popularity:

- Free Fire has over 1 billion downloads on the Google Play Store alone, making it one of
  the most downloaded mobile games of all time.
- The game has an estimated 150-200 million monthly active users worldwide, with a strong
  presence in Asia, Latin America, and other regions.
- Free Fire has a strong esports scene, with numerous tournaments and competitions held
  globally, including the Free Fire World Series.

Revenue and Monetization:

- Free Fire generates revenue through in-app purchases, including diamonds (the game's 
 premium currency), characters, and other virtual items.
- The game also has a subscription-based model, called "Free Fire Pass," which offers 
 exclusive rewards and benefits.

Updates and Collaborations:

- Free Fire receives regular updates with new content, events, and game modes.
- The game has collaborated with popular franchises like McLaren, Ferrari, and Marvel,
  introducing exclusive content and themed events.
- Free Fire has also partnered with various influencers and streamers, further increasing 
 its popularity.

Awards and Recognition:

- Free Fire won the "Best Popular Vote Game" award at the 2019 Google Play Awards.
- The game was also nominated for "Best Mobile Game" at the 2020 Golden Joystick Awards.
